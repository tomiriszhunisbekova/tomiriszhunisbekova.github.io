Lake Tahoe, California

I went to Lake Tahoe, California for a Work and Travel program. I spent my summer near the beautiful lake, working at the Ritz-Carlton Lake Tahoe. I have acquired a great amount of professional skills while working at the Front Desk Department. As well as this I travelled to other cities and states as you can see below.

Las Vegas, Nevada

My first destination was Las Vegas, Nevada. Nevada's main city and economic hub is Las Vegas. Las Vegas is a city of spectacular architecture and signs with a million lights, as well as obvious affluence and cleverly concealed poverty. Superlatives, both good and negative, abound there.

Disneyland, Los Angeles, California

They say dreams come true. Well, mine definitely did when I visited Disneyland in Los Angeles. Even to spend 17 hours there was a true joy. Walt's first Theme Park offers everlasting amusement with a blend of modern and vintage attractions. Some of your favorite Disney characters may be met here.

Los Angeles, California

Los Angeles, city of Angels. Los Angeles, the hub of the country's film and television industry, is a large metropolis in Southern California. Studios including Paramount Pictures, Universal, and Warner Brothers provide behind-the-scenes tours close to the famous Hollywood sign. I enjoyed my adventure there, even though this is one of the most dangerous cities in the world.
