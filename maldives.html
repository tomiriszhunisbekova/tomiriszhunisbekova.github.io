Maldives

I would describe Maldives as beautiful nature with a great cultural heritage that is full of coconut. The Maldives are well-known for its white sand beaches, crystal-clear seas, and distinctive underwater marine life. The Maldives have two dominant monsoons that contribute to its year-round warm and humid tropical climate. There are around 200 inhabited islands in the Maldives, and the population is distributed widely among the atolls. The other islands are either deserted or utilized for agriculture and other forms of subsistence. Approximately 164 islands have been developed as tourist destinations. Male is the Maldives' national capital.
